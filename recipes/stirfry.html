Stir Fry

Description

This is a simple stirfry. Specific ingredients dont matter all that much, just use whatver meat (or meat substitute) you'd like, as well as whatever veggies you like best!

Ingredients

  • Meat, or meat substitie
  • Veggies (carrots, snow peas, tomatoes, brocolli, bell peppers, etc.)
  • Garlic, ginger, celery, onion, all minced
  • Soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, siracha sauce, any other sauce or seasoning you'd like

Steps

  1. Prep everything! Chop veggies into appropriate size, meat into bite size pieces
  2. Mince garlic, onion, ginger, celery
  3. Heat wok with oil, brown meat, then remove
  4. Saute garlic, onion, celery and ginger for 1-2 min.
  5. Add other veggies, saute for 5-10 min.
  6. Add meat, cook for a few more min., until meant is done
  7. Add seasonings, stir and serve over rice, quinoa, or whatever you'd like
