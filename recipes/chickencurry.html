Chicken Curry

Description

The best and simplest chicken curry you will ever make! Just a few ingredients are necessary:

  • Chicken
  • Curry powder (and cayenne if you like it hot)
  • Chicken broth
  • Garlic, ginger, onions (all minced)
  • Any ther veggies/beans you would like
  • A spoonful of plain yogurt

Steps

  1. Chop chicken into bite sized pieces
  2. Mince garlic, ginger, and onions
  3. Measure 2 tblsp curry powder, 1 tsp cayenne
  4. Chop any other veggies (carrots, potatoes, etc.)
  5. Brown meat in a med. size pot
  6. Remove, and saute onion in same pot
  7. Add spices and ginger/garlic, heat for 1 min
  8. Add broth, add meat and veggies. Makes sure broth almost covers everything
  9. Bring to a boil, turn down heat, cover let simmer for ~1hr.
  10. When meat is tender, add yogurt
  11. Serve over rice, or quinoa, or by itself. However you like!
